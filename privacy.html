Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for UI Designer Pro ("we," "our," or "the Application"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and security. This document outlines how our application interacts with your Roblox account when you authorize it through the Roblox OAuth 2.0 system.

By installing the UI Designer Pro plugin in Roblox Studio, using the Figma paired plugin, and linking your account via the OAuth 2.0 application, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Roblox OAuth Scopes and Permissions

To enable asset uploads directly from your design environment to your Roblox account, UI Designer Pro requests the following permissions from Roblox:

  • assets:read: Allows the application to retrieve a list of existing assets in your inventory. We use this to verify if an asset is already uploaded, preventing duplicates and allowing asset reuse.
  • assets:write: Allows the application to upload newly generated user interface images, decals, and visual files directly to your Roblox account on your behalf.

🔒 Scope Limit and Safety

These scopes are strictly limited. UI Designer Pro cannot access your private credentials (passwords, 2FA codes), read your email, buy or trade items, spend Robux, access group funds, or edit scripts and maps inside your places.

3. Data Transit & Ephemeral Storage

We operate under a strict Zero-Retention Policy. The technical infrastructure of the Application is designed to ensure maximum privacy:

  1. No Database Storage: We do not operate, own, or rent any databases. No user information, access tokens, or uploaded file data is ever saved or written to persistent storage.
  2. In-Transit Processing: When you execute an upload request from the Figma Plugin, the local plugin routes the asset payload and the secure OAuth access token to a serverless Cloudflare Worker.
  3. Immediate Deletion: The Cloudflare Worker processes the token and asset purely in volatile memory (RAM) to sign and forward the request to the official Roblox API. Once the Roblox API responds, the Worker terminates, and all information is immediately erased from memory.
  4. No Developer Access: Because the processing is ephemeral and no database exists, the developer/owner of UI Designer Pro has zero visibility or access to your tokens, files, or Roblox profile information. Think of the process as handing a letter to a mailman, and having them deliver the contents (Images Or Decals) to another home (Roblox), our product (The Mailman) does not ever open said package.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information during transit:

  • Encryption: All communication between the Figma plugin, the Cloudflare Worker proxy, and the Roblox API endpoints is fully encrypted using secure Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTPS / SSL/TLS).
  • Local Token Cache: The OAuth token returned by Roblox is stored securely inside Figma's local secure plugin storage or Roblox Studio's settings cache on your local computer. It is never exposed to external hosts other than the secure Cloudflare Worker proxy.

5. Third-Party Services

Because UI Designer Pro integrates directly into Roblox, your use of the application is also governed by the policies of:

  • Roblox Corporation: Assets uploaded are subject to the Roblox Terms of Use and Community Standards. You can review their privacy policy at the Roblox Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare: The proxy processing is hosted on Cloudflare Workers. You can review their privacy policy at the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

6. How to Revoke Access

You are in complete control of your account access. You can disconnect UI Designer Pro from your Roblox account at any time:

  1. Log in to your account on the official Roblox.com website.
  2. Navigate to your Settings page.
  3. Select the Security tab.
  4. Scroll down to the App Permissions or authorized applications section.
  5. Locate UI Designer Pro and click Revoke Access.

Once access is revoked, the OAuth tokens cached on your computer will be immediately invalidated and cannot be used to read or write assets anymore.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We recommend checking this page periodically to remain informed about our data protection standards.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the security of UI Designer Pro, please contact the plugin developer via email or open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
