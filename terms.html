2. Description of Service

UI Designer Pro is a developmental utility plugin. It allows creators to authorize their Roblox accounts via OAuth 2.0 to upload local interface designs such as decals, images, and other assets directly onto their Roblox account, where they will then be instanced into their Roblox Studio environment with the active plugin.
